# Game Design Document: MVP Specifications

## 1. Multiplayer & Technical Core
- **Mode:** Multi-player enabled.
- **Classes:** Three playable classes.
    - Each must have 10 functional abilities designed and working.
    - Unique graphics for each class.
    - Class Differentiation: Clear specialties (Pros/Cons) to encourage diverse playstyles.

## 2. Combat System
- **Targeting:** Must include a functional targeting system.
- **Progression:** Functioning Experience (EXP) system and Monster tables.
- **Economy:** Functioning Loot tables.
- **Cooperation:** Must include cooperative effects (Buffs/Debuffs).

## 3. World & Mini-Campaign
### Mini-Campaign Requirements:
- Minimum of four complete canvases.
- **Progression:** A natural quest line from beginning to end.
- **Goal:** Clear end goal with a unique treasure reward.
- **Narrative:** Integrated lore.

### Map Elements:
- **Capital City (One per canvas):**
    - Inn (Healing)
    - Weapons Shop
    - Quest Board
    - Specialty Shops
    - Larger tile count than towns.
    - *Non-MVP: Police/Security, Fast Travel.*
- **Towns ("A few" per canvas):**
    - Inn (Healing)
    - Limited shops.
    - Fewer NPCs than capitals.

### Starter Dungeons:
- Single-canvas maps.
- No story requirement.
- Treasure at the end.

## 4. Data Tables
- Comprehensive Monster tables.
- Comprehensive Loot tables.
